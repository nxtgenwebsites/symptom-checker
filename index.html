Summariser

This AI Summariser gives you the option to discuss your symptoms fully in private in a conversation. It will ask you questions, and you can ask questions as well. Based on these conversations, it will understand your symptoms and create a comprehensive medical summary of the symptoms you describe for you to approve.

The summarised report will be saved in your account, and you can delete it at any time. You will be able to share it with multiple healthcare providers for consistent health assistance.
